IT specialist from Poznań who codes by one overriding principle: user data belongs exclusively to the user.

RAIDO Development is a solo project — every line of code mine, every design decision intentional. I build tools that do exactly what they say. No dark patterns, no asking for access to things they don't need.

I use AI tools for development — Anthropic Claude and Google AI — treating them as assistants, not a replacement for my own judgment. Every deployment is preceded by thorough testing and quality verification.

Beyond the screen — a motorcycle, heavy music, and the conviction that the best tools don't ask who you are.

Philosophy

Mission

"Your data is your data. Not ours. Not the servers'. Not the advertisers'."

Privacy

Offline-first. Data stays on your device. No accounts, no registration, no hidden connections to external servers.

Control

Export your data whenever you want, in a format you understand. Delete it — it's truly gone. No hidden layer.

Simplicity

Apps do exactly what they say. No dark patterns, no asking for access to things they don't need.
